# Waste Related Complaint Management System

## Overview
The **Waste Related Complaint Management System** is a web-based application designed to manage, track, and streamline complaints related to waste management in urban or residential areas.  
The system connects **citizens**, **municipal staff/collection centers**, and **administrators** to ensure timely resolution of complaints and proper waste handling.

This project focuses on **efficient workflow management**, **data transparency**, and **role-based access**, making it a practical example of a real-world backend system.

---

## Problem Statement
Inefficient handling of waste complaints leads to:
- Accumulation of uncollected waste
- Health hazards and environmental pollution
- Low accountability in municipal services

Manual tracking of complaints is slow, error-prone, and lacks transparency.  
This system digitizes the complaint process, ensuring **accountability, timely resolution, and proper monitoring**.

---

## Features

### User Module
- User registration and authentication
- Submit waste-related complaints (e.g., uncollected garbage, illegal dumping)
- Track complaint status (Pending / In Progress / Resolved)
- View complaint history and feedback

### Municipal Staff / Collection Center Module
- View assigned complaints
- Update complaint status and progress
- Maintain records of resolved complaints

### Admin Module
- Manage users and municipal staff accounts
- Assign complaints and monitor resolution timelines
- Analyze overall waste management statistics
- Generate reports and insights for decision-making

---

## System Roles
- **User**: Reports waste-related issues and tracks resolutions  
- **Municipal Staff / Collection Center**: Handles complaints, updates status, and ensures resolution  
- **Admin**: Oversees the system, assigns complaints, monitors performance, and generates reports
